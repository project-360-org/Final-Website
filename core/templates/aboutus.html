Our Missions and Visions


The mission of Equity Bridge Nepal is to democratize Information on career paths and self-paced learning resources and develop soft-skills among high school students through interactive classroom exercises. Our long term vision is to accelerate the process of minimizing systemic inequity in educational outcomes.

Motivation

"Soft skills" and information on various career opportunities are critical to achieving job/grant/college outcomes after having passed the hurdle of developing core knowledge in a particular subject. Role models are also crucial in providing our youth the right guidance and confidence required at various crossroads of life.

Our Model

We aim to digitize two things:

1. Classroom teaching of soft-skills through interactive in-person games (Train-the-Trainer Model) with complementary online content
2. Early career exposure to students and talent identification for companies, primarily democratizing the following household questions:

  • Career मा स्कोप के छ?
  • के के सिक्न पर्छ?
  • कसरी सफल बन्ने?
