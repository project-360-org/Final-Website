Project Sapana

With support from the Davis Peace Foundation award, a successful pilot program (Project Sapana) focused on teaching valuable soft skills to 17 higher secondary students at Kankali Secondary School, a government school outside of Kathmandu.

Students were also exposed to shadowing and career exposure opportunities from executives and senior management at three innovative Nepali technology companies: F1Soft Group, GrowbyData, and Bhoos. This provided them with crucial information and role models to look up to when they had just graduated high school.
